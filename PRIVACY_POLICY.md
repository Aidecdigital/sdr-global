# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 14, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SDR Global ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the SDR Global website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with us.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Contact Information**: When you submit contact forms, apply for positions, or request partnerships, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide.
- **Application Materials**: When you apply for opportunities, we collect your resume, cover letter, and application responses.
- **Direct Communications**: When you email us or contact us, we keep your messages and our responses.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Respond to inquiries** and fulfill your requests
- **Process applications** and contact you about opportunities
- **Communicate** with you via email when you interact with us
- **Comply with legal obligations**

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only:

- **With service providers** who help us operate (hosting, email services, etc.)
- **When required by law** or to protect our legal rights
- **With partners** when you specifically request partnership information

## 5. Data Retention

- **Contact forms**: Kept for 2 years
- **Applications and resumes**: Kept per employment law requirements
- **Email communications**: Kept for 1 year after last contact

## 6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable precautions.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- **Access** your personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your information (with some exceptions)
- **Opt-out** of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@sdrglobal.io

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 9. Third-Party Links

Our website links to third-party sites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.
